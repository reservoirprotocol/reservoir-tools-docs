title: What is Reservoir?
description: "Tools for Token Trading on Every Chain"
---

Reservoir is a suite of developer tools designed to help wallets, apps, and chains build with tokens. We specialize in cross-chain token liquidity and onchain token trading infrastructure.

_Our goal is make **every token on every chain tradable** and deploy reliable decentralized exchange infrastructure for tokens and NFTs._
